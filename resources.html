neat resources

hello quizzers! this page contains various resources. see below:

these resources were compiled by emma hermacinski! contact her here or on discord with any questions!

  • this is the choate protobowl room. we’re going to have scheduled times for group protobowl thirty minutes after curfew each night [2130-2200 on school nights, 2300-2330 on fridays, 2330-0000 on saturdays]. whether you can/want to go to these or not, protobowl is key to quiz bowl practice--it’s a program that will read you around 70k different high school quiz bowl questions!
  • a website with basically all the major non-naqt packets from the past twenty years. great to read if you want to get a feel for tossups, but please only read the even packets as we practice with the odd ones.
  • a website that allows you to search for any term and see what questions it comes up in.
  • the stanford culture index is a big list of all of the terms that would come up ever. definitely more college-level, but super useful.
  • the official rules for pace gameplay
  • a list of important answers that come up in certain categories. we’re also doing presentations on these during meetings throughout the
    • term!
  • a youtube playlist of ted videos that explain various myths from around the world, a great way to study mythology. [thanks to andrew lee for pointing to this]
  • if you’re planning on studying mythology, this is the website for you. good stuff on religion too.
  • a series of short science lectures that explain concepts across all scientific fields. [big thanks to andrew lee for this]
  • this is a folder of short study guides on various topics made by ace, a quiz bowl organization. history players, check out the history bee notes [exceedingly helpful].
  • arguably andrew lee’s magnum opus--science players, look no further. this is a compilation of all textbooks and information needed to be a boss at science quiz bowl. also good for writing questions!
  • if you want to meet other people doing quiz bowl, get advice, or just ask questions, this is the link to the official quiz bowl forums! my [emma’s] username on there is herm, so friend me!
  • an explanation of the quiz bowl lingo, so that you, too, may speak like a radical dude.
  • this is a big document with all of the recommended study resources and books to read to get good at quiz bowl. definitely check it out.
  • big advice from a quiz bowl lad.
  • a very comprehensive list of quiz bowl flashcards.
  • for if you go to a tournament. this is an explanation of all the different stats for you and your team.
